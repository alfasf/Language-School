Services

Our school facilities offer available resources to our students to enhance their learning at no cost for some of the services. Library, cafeteria and translation services are opened to the community

Guardabarranco Cafeteria

It offers a variety of pastries, snacks, meals, lunches, brunch, hot and cold beverages.

Guardabarranco Cafeteria Schedule
Mon thru Sat: 11am to 9pm Sundays: 11am to 5pm

Computer Labs

Students have access to the our 3 computer labs with the latest technology for their language learning like voice recognition and accent reduction software, among other things. Each student is given with an account for their use of computer labs, printers and copiers.

Library

In addition to computer labs, our library welcomes students and the community as extension of their English learning. With a collection of 1,000 titles among books, magazines, journals, encyclopedia and videos, it serves the community for research. The library also serves as space to students to study. Registered students can borrow materials for a period of time.

Translations

ILA offers translation services to the community for a reasonable fee. Translations are provided for experienced translators on the field for your lawyers, medical, academic, technical and other purposes. Translations provided by ILA are certified by International Federation of Translators, which are used by government, scientific and legal documents around the world, including Nicaragua.
